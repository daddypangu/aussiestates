About Kalamunda

Kalamunda has a population of 58,762; 49.5% male and 50.5% female. The median age of Kalamunda residents is 40, and median weekly personal income was $835. Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander people made up 2.3% of the population.

Area: 10.6 km² (4.1 sq mi)
Location: 25 km (16 mi) from Perth
Population: 7,163 (SAL 2021)
